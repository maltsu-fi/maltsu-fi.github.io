Policy for maltsu.fi
Last-Updated: 2025-11-26

1. Purpose
The domain maltsu.fi is operated solely for technical and security research purposes.
No commercial services are offered from this domain.

2. Good-Faith Research
Good-faith security research and responsible disclosure are welcomed. Reports that are
submitted responsibly, without causing harm or disruption, will be treated with respect
and handled confidentially.

3. Authorized Testing
This policy does NOT grant permission to:
- Perform unauthorized access
- Attempt exploitation
- Conduct denial-of-service attacks
- Perform intrusive scanning against third-party systems
- Intercept or modify traffic belonging to others

Only systems explicitly designated by the operator for testing are in scope. At present,
no public attack surface is intentionally exposed for testing.

4. Out-of-Scope Activities
The following activities are strictly out of scope and not authorized:
- Data exfiltration
- Privilege escalation
- Service disruption
- Attacks against infrastructure not owned or controlled by the operator
- Attacks using maltsu.fi as an origin to target others

5. Reporting
If you discover a security issue, abuse, or anomalous activity related to maltsu.fi,
please report it responsibly according to https://maltsu.fi/.well-known/security.txt

Reports should include, where possible:
- Date and time (with timezone)
- Source and destination IP addresses
- Relevant logs or packet captures
- A concise technical description

6. Disclosure Handling
Reports will be reviewed and triaged in a reasonable timeframe. Coordinated disclosure
is preferred. No bug bounty program is currently offered.

7. Privacy
Reporter identity will be handled confidentially and only shared when required by law
or with explicit consent.

8. Safe Testing Statement
This policy is intended to support responsible research and reporting. It does not
authorize any activity that would be unlawful under applicable local, national, or
international law.
